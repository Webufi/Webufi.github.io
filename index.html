Dance, art and music isn’t just putting steps, paint & cords together – It is SO much more! It’s knowing how to take those steps you are taught and making them your OWN!

The same applies to life itself – One can be taught endless lessons but it up to the individual to interpret and apply those lessons and apply those lessons. To achieve this one might have to overcome personal obstacles including self doubt, negative influence & fear, to name a few.

At i-Move our sole purpose is to guide young leaders to overcome any negative factors. We look for the UNIQUE creative inside each young heart. Working through a carefully crafted program, we allow each one to step out of their shell and become the leader he/she is born to be.  

We have created a safe place for all young leaders to explore & discover their inner voice, build confidence and unlock powerful leadership skills.

This isn’t just a dance / drama class … This is about self exploration, personal interpretation, building self esteem and embracing what makes one unique.

At i-Move we celebrate each individuals contribution and encourage creative confidence – while practicing positive self talk all the way.

To be great, you first need to be you!

Performance Class An introduction to the vast array of music genres and how to recognize them. It is important understand the effect music / sound can have on our emotions. It is also important to understand how one can use music to express emotion. We use age appropriate music to educate and assist our young leaders in their journey of growth with us.
Music 

Performance is all about the personal interpretation of music, art and dance. Here we guide our leaders to experiment with the limitations of their own body & creativity. They are shown how to take charge & to trust their unique ideas and bodies. Each leader is celebrated for their contribution and given free rein on interpretation.

A positive sense of self is one of the greatest gifts you can give a child. Children with high self-esteem feel loved and competent and develop into happy, productive people. The ability to build trust in others is just as essential to our wellbeing and to our success in life. Here we give our leaders stepping stones to allow them to trust themselves and their team.

Have you ever heard a piece of music that just makes you want to get up and move? During our program we offer our leaders just that. A chance to bring music interpretation, dance & art together. Don’t underestimate the power of creativity when given the chance to mix it all up. The canvass becomes the stage & their voice to the world.

Whatever one does in life, strength, flexibility & control through breathing is essential. Core exercise and stretching is a part of every class. This not only improves posture but also aids in better focus in school & everyday life. On top of this is the impact of positive self talk. Here we show each and every one the importance of how we talk to ourselves. We also practice the use of mindfulness in each class.

A bit on the Founder:

Anyone who knows Tammy will be able to verify her flare for the dramatic, as well as her undying love for art in all forms.

Being a “free spirit”, Tammy was naturally drawn to the performing arts early on in life. She studied at the renowned Waterfront Theatre College for the Performing Arts, graduating with a diploma in Musical Theatre. Thereafter she spent many exciting years travelling locally and abroad with some fascinating stage productions.
